Hello!

I'm Lukas Lubingo, a passionate web designer and front-end developer with a love for creating visually stunning and functional websites.
My journey into web design began in 2017 when I dived into the world of WIX studio after completing high-school. The initial spark ignited my curiosity to understand the mechanics behind the captivating user interfaces I encountered. This led me to enroll in a boot camp where I delved into the fundamentals of HTML and CSS. As I started building websites, I found myself drawn deeper into the art of web design.
I expanded my skills by self-learning JavaScript and Bootstrap to enhance the functionality of my creations. Now, I am eager to take on new challenges and opportunities in the realm of web design and front-end development.
